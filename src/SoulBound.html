Soulbound Sheets

That might not be the best name ever, but Life Ledger or Titans Tome did not quite carry the message. It's an app for character sheets for Soulbound.

What exactly is that about?

Soulbound is a RPG set in Age of Sigmar, an universe belonging to Games Workshop's huge Warhammer franchise. I run Undead hordes for Nagash, Skaven multitudes for the Great Horned Rat, Squigs and Bad Moon Clans when it suits the Gitz, and there are T'au, Salamanders, Grey Knights, Custodes, Necromunda, Blood Bowl, WarCry tribes and so many more all over our shelves.

Yes. We like the universes Games Workshop build. And so, we needed a way to store our super-epic characters, because, yes, Soulbound is about being super-epic in a world that is so epic even super-epic characters have a short life expectancy. It's the big idea. Fight hard, win glory, die an wondertastic warrior's death.

- OK then. So it's a RPG, you play with dice and paper and you want an app for storing characters. Anything else?

- Why, yes. Great luck that you should ask. It's also used to store notes about the campaigns, adventures, and sessions. What's the point of a character app if it doesn't store their adventures?

It's also a way for me to sharpen some of my favorite skills with software testing and iOS programming.
